About CampusConnect

CampusConnect is a student-built platform designed to simplify campus life. Whether you're trying to find a lost item, exchange books, buy second-hand electronics, or explore hostel essentials — this site brings it all together in one place.

Our mission is to help students connect and support each other by creating a digital marketplace and lost-and-found hub tailored to campus needs. It's easy to use, open to everyone in the college, and completely free.

With categories ranging from books, electronics, fashion, and hostel stuff to events and stationery, CampusConnect serves as your one-stop solution for everyday campus life. You can also report lost/found items, post ads, or browse what's available.

Created as a final year BCA project, this website is not just a submission — it’s a real-time tool meant to be helpful for students like us. We hope it grows into something bigger with your support!
